FAQ

Food and Drinks

People from the school where the event is taking place will sell drinks, sandwiches and fries at a very reasonable price. There're also some places to eat in the neighborhood. An overview can be found here : http://loadays.org/places-eat

Infrastructure

We'll provide internet access for all visitors. Keep in mind that we don't have a state of the art WIFI network. There will also be a place where you can connect your laptop to the wired network and power outlets. But bring your own cable.
